### 8th Grade Math Practice Assessment

---

**Question 1**
Solve the following equation for x:
3/4 x - 5 = 1/2(x + 6)
A) x = 44
B) x = 32
C) x = 16
D) x = 22
**Skill**: 8.EE.C.7.b (Solve linear equations with rational number coefficients.)

---

**Question 2**
Which of the following relations represents a function?
A) {(2, 3), (2, 4), (3, 5)}
B) {(1, 4), (2, 4), (3, 4)}
C) {(0, 1), (1, 2), (0, 3)}
D) {(5, 1), (6, 2), (5, 3)}
**Skill**: 8.F.A.1 (Understand that a function is a rule that assigns to each input exactly one output.)

---

**Question 3**
A right triangle has a perpendicular height of 9 cm and a base of 12 cm. What is the length of the hypotenuse?
A) 15 cm
B) 21 cm
C) 12 cm
D) 81 cm
**Skill**: 8.G.B.7 (Apply the Pythagorean Theorem to determine unknown side lengths in right triangles.)

---

**Question 4**
A teacher plots the number of hours students spent studying versus their test scores on a scatter plot. What type of association would you most likely expect to see?
A) Strong negative association
B) No association
C) Strong positive association
D) Non-linear association
**Skill**: 8.SP.A.1 (Construct and interpret scatter plots for bivariate measurement data.)

---

**Question 5**
Solve for y: 
0.5(2y - 4) = 3y + 8
A) y = -5
B) y = -10
C) y = 5
D) y = 0
**Skill**: 8.EE.C.7.b (Solve linear equations with rational number coefficients.)

---

**Question 6**
A 13-foot ladder is leaning against a wall. The base of the ladder is 5 feet away from the wall. How high up the wall does the ladder reach?
A) 8 feet
B) 12 feet
C) 144 feet
D) 10 feet
**Skill**: 8.G.B.7 (Apply the Pythagorean Theorem to determine unknown side lengths in right triangles.)

---

**Answer Key**:
1. A
2. B
3. A
4. C
5. A
6. B
